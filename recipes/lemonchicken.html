Lemon Seasoned Chicken Thighs

Description

This is a very simple recipe, but can take a while. This recipe combines lemon seasoning and other flavors to make a delicious, and extremely healthy alternative to other chicken that anyone will love! This is personally one of my favorite recipes out there, and it is by far my favorite dinner to have every night!

Ingredients Needed
  • 5-6 russet potatoes peeled and sliced
  • 8 bone in skin on chicken thighs
  • 2 juiced lemons
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1-2 tsp Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 tsp minced garlic with red chili peppers
Instructions
  1. In a bowl add in lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic with red peppers, oregano, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, and whisk everything together (taste and adjust as needed).
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut into wedges and add them to a 9 x 13 baking dish.
  3. Pour half of your lemon oil mixture over the potatoes. Toss.
  4. Add chicken thighs evenly over the top of the potatoes.
  5. Season chicken with garlic powder, paprika, Italian herb seasoning, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp pepper.
  6. Over the chicken pour the rest of the lemon oil mixture evenly.
  7. Bake in the oven at 375 degrees for 1 hour.
  8. Take chicken out of the pan and put potatoes back in the over on broil until they are slightly browned.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
